Sri Pada Sri Vallabha Temple

The Sacred Abode of Lord Dattatreya's First Incarnation

Divine Significance

First Incarnation of Lord Dattatreya

Sri Pada Sri Vallabha is revered as the first incarnation of Lord Dattatreya, the divine trinity of Brahma, Vishnu, and Maheshwara. The temple marks the sacred birthplace of this divine incarnation, who blessed the world with his presence in the 14th century.

Historical Legacy

Divine Birth

  • Born to Sri Appala Raju Sharma and Sumati Mata in Pithapuram
  • Exhibited miraculous powers from childhood
  • Blessed countless devotees with spiritual guidance

Sacred Text

  • "Sri Pada Sri Vallabha Charitamrutam" chronicles his divine life
  • Contains miraculous incidents and teachings
  • Revered as a spiritual guide by devotees

Temple Architecture

Sacred Shrine

  • Life-size idol of Sri Vallabha Swamy in meditation
  • Sanctum radiates divine peace and serenity
  • Traditional temple architecture with intricate carvings

Temple Complex

  • Shrines dedicated to Lord Dattatreya and Anagha Devi
  • Birth site marked as "Swayambhu Kshetra"
  • Peaceful meditation spaces

Spiritual Practices

Daily Rituals

  • Traditional Abhishekam and Alankaram

  • Sacred text recitation

Special Celebrations

  • Special full moon day ceremonies
