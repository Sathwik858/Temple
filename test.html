Kukkuteswara Swamy Temple

Temple Overview

  • Located in Central Pithapuram
  • Ancient Shiva temple featuring the sacred Padagaya Sarovar
  • Mythologically connected to Lord Vishnu's Matsya avatar

Darshan Timings

  • Morning: 5:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Evening: 4:30 PM – 8:30 PM

Available Sevas

  • Shiva Kalyanam - ₹558 (Pournami Day, 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM)
  • Chandi Homam - ₹1,116 (Pournami Day, 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM)
  • Abhishekam - ₹100 (Daily, 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM)
  • Special Archana - ₹50

Sri Pada Sri Vallabha Temple

Temple Overview

  • Located in North Pithapuram
  • Dedicated to the first avatar of Lord Dattatreya
  • Renowned for its serene meditation ambiance

Darshan Timings

  • Morning: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Available Sevas

  • Sri Pada Charitamrutam Parayanam (Daily at 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM)
  • Datta Homam - ₹2,000 (Conducted on auspicious occasions)
  • Special Archana to Sri Vallabha Swamy - ₹100

Kunti Madhava Swamy Temple

Temple Overview

  • Located in East Pithapuram
  • Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, established by Kunti Devi
  • Known for its historical and spiritual significance

Darshan Timings

  • Morning: 6:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Evening: 4:30 PM – 8:30 PM

Available Sevas

  • Vishnu Sahasranama Archana - ₹50 (Daily at 7:00 AM)
  • Thirumanjanam - ₹150 (Conducted every Saturday)
  • Satyanarayana Vratham - ₹516 (Performed on Full Moon days)

Shri Gopal Baba Maharaj Ashram

Overview

  • Located in Pithapuram outskirts
  • Dedicated to Shri Gopal Baba, revered as an incarnation of Shripad Shri Vallabha Swamy
  • A peaceful ashram for meditation and spiritual upliftment

Darshan Timings

  • Morning: 5:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Available Sevas

  • Guru Pooja (Conducted daily at 6:00 AM and 7:00 PM)
  • Anagha Vratam - ₹501 (Monthly pooja for family well-being)
  • Annadanam (Food Donation - Cost varies by choice)
